About the Event

AG Christmas beach volley tounament is a one-day volleyball festival featuring competitive brackets, community activities. Intermediate-skill level ("B") welcome. Matches held on indoor beach courts.

  • When: December 7, 2025
  • Where: Hiekka Beach Volley club
  • Courts: 2, 3 and 4
  • Divisions: Mixed (M+W)
